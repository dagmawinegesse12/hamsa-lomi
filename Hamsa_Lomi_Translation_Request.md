### About paragraphs (translate each)

**Paragraph 1:**
In the heart of Nashville, Tennessee, the Ethiopian community recognized a profound need to create a self-help association that could provide comfort and financial support to those facing the loss of a loved one. Rooted in Ethiopian culture, Hamsa Lomi was established to ease the financial burden of funeral expenses while offering emotional and personal assistance during the mourning process.

**Paragraph 2:**
Founded in 2014 by dedicated Ethiopian volunteers in Nashville, Hamsa Lomi embodies the rich traditions of communal care and mutual support that have been passed down through generations in Ethiopia. From its inception, the association has been guided by a strong By-law, with seven elected Board members overseeing its operations and ensuring the organization serves its mission effectively.

**Paragraph 3:**
